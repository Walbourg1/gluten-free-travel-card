2. Data we collect

2.1 No personal data collected through use of the app

Gluten Free Travel Card does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

  • We do not require you to create an account or log in
  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any contact information
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not use analytics tools (e.g. Google Analytics, Firebase)
  • We do not use advertising networks or tracking SDKs
  • We do not share any data with third parties

2.2 Data stored locally on your device

The App stores the following data exclusively on your device:

  • App preferences (e.g. favourite languages, font size)
  • Onboarding completion status

This data never leaves your device and is automatically deleted when you uninstall the App.

2.3 Legal basis (Art. 6 GDPR)

Since all data is processed and stored exclusively on your device and never reaches us, it does not constitute processing by us within the meaning of the GDPR.

3. Text-to-speech (Read Aloud)

The "Read Aloud" function uses your device's built-in text-to-speech engine provided by Apple (iOS) or Google (Android). Text is processed locally on your device. We do not send any text to external servers.

4. Donations (PayPal)

If you choose to make a donation via PayPal, you are redirected to PayPal's website. Any data you provide during that process is subject to PayPal's own privacy policy. We do not receive or store your payment details.

5. Children's privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13.

6. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The current version is always available at this URL.
